vision and mission

ZILLA PARISHAD HIGH SCHOOL

VISION

To ensure free and quality education across all state-run government schools. To ensure a bright future for the children and to bring back the glorious old days of public education.

MISSION

To achieve our vision, it is imperative that we build a people’s movement, by creating awareness about the importance of education and the fact that it is the state government’s duty to provide quality education in all public schools We also aim to ensure a high standard of public education and the need to bring in transparency and accountability in the overarching education system. We work to build leaders in the field of education who will work towards building sustainable efforts in transforming the educational system in AP.
